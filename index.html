About David:

My name is David Sanderson, I was born in Colorado Springs, Colorado, in the year of 1890, in the midst of the Gold Rush. Early in my life, I decided to move south and call Albuquerque, New Mexico my home. My father was a mountain man, the last of the great giants of the Rockies. My mother, on the other hand, was an adventurous mountaineer with a heart of gold. Even here, so far away from the magnificent peaks, I still hear the song of the mountains ringing in my soul.

Some of my favorite things include:
  • Reading
  • Learning
  • Hiking
  • Hunting with my Dogs
  • Drinking from fresh mountain streams
  • Stargazing
  • An axe in my hands, and laughter in my heart

Place of Origin: Colorado Springs, Colorado.

I learned at Mother Nature's Busom all that a man need know

I consider myself a thinking man, a man that contemplates the important things of life. My grandfather used to tell me that he who cannot still himself, and feel the wind whispering in his ear has lost himself to the modern world
